What You Need To Know About CklineHMO

CKLINE Healthcare Limited is a limited liability company that is duly registered to underwrite health insurance in Nigeria. The company is being managed by seasoned professionals with proven track records in the Managed Healthcare Service and Health Insurance Industry, in that we have provider contracts with over 400 other clinics/hospitals nationwide, which geographically spread over 126 Nigerian cities. Our list of hospitals is unlimited to this, we are open to update any good NHIS hospitals of choice not listed in our providers network,
We also run staff Pre-Employment test and manage outsourced worksite clinics health centers nationwide, Quality delivery of managed care services in a professional fee-for-service system.

-No matter your budget we have a suitable plan for your health needs.

-Vast network of providers nationwide. Branded health Access Card for corporate organization.

but must not exceed four (4) members and must not be more than 22 years of age,
all other dependants and individuals such as another wife, other children,
other domestic help and other family members that are under the principal insured
will fall under our private policy or registered separately.
